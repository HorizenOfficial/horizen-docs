---
title: "Multisig Wallets"
---

Den provides a self-custodial, multi-signature wallet for onchain organizations on Horizen. It is is built on top of the Safe Contracts, which is the most trusted asset storage mechanism onchain.

You can explore more about the Den deployed on Horizen on [this dashboard](https://safe.horizen.io/welcome).
